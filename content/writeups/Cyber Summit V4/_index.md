During the **CyberTrace V4.0 CTF**, I had the opportunity to contribute as a challenge author, designing and developing tasks across multiple categories, including `Cryptography, Binary Exploitation, Forensics, Misc, and Special.`

In this section, I document the intended solutions for each challenge, the design choices behind them, common pitfalls encountered by players, and the techniques required to reach the flags.

`These writeups serve both as a reference for participants and as a resource for anyone interested in understanding how CTF challenges are built and solved.`

Thanks to the **CyberTrace** team for organizing such an engaging and educational event 🚀
